Categories of Charities

Select which organizations to support by exploring the various categories

Education

These charaties create educational oppportunities for areas where quality education may be scarce, transforming the futures of underpriveldged people.

Environment

These charities focus their efforts on restoring damaged natural systems which humans rely on, consequently preserving natural beauty.

Health

The main purpose of these charities is to take action towards improving health and wellbeing across the globe, including those affected by various outbreaks and diseases.

Animals

Animal charities are classified by the organizations that have been established to improve global welfare standards for animals.

Global

Charities in this category help those effected by various foriegn problems, including the aid those fleeing persecution and helping countries hit by devastating situations.

Humanitarian

Humanitarian charities are categorized by those that take innnovative approaches to human rights problems regardless of borders.
